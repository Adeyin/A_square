GPA Calculator manual
Click "View my course marks": view the course record

  • Click "Academic advices"
  • Input current year semester
  • Input Target tga
  • Click "Calculate grade point required to meet my target tga" to get the result
  • Click "reset" to reset the input
Click "Add course record: add new course information
  • Input the information of course
  • Input Year, Semester, Course code, No of credits, grade
  • Click "Add next course" to add new course
  • Click "finish" to finish the input
  • Click "reset" to reset the input

Click "Delete all record": delete all the record

Click "My TGA": Term Grade Average

  • TGA is calculated across all courses taken in a term.

Click "My CGA": Cumulative Grade Average

  • CGA is calculated for all the courses earning grade points completed since first registration in a program up to the point of calculation.

Click "My GGA": Graduation Cumulative Grade Average

  • GGA is the student's CGA adjusted by excluding courses and adopting a weighting policy under established University's rules.
