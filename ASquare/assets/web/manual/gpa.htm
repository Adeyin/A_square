GPA Calcolator manual
Click "View my course marks": view the course record

  1. Click "Academic advices"
  2. Input current year semester
  3. Input Target tga
  4. Click "Calcolate grade point required to meet my target tga" to get the resolt
  5. Click "reset" to reset the input
Click "Add course record: add new course information
  1. Input the information of course
  2. Input Year, Semester, Course code, No of credits, grade
  3. Click "Add next course" to add new course
  4. Click "finish" to finish the input
  5. Click "reset" to reset the input

Click "Delete all record": delete all the record

Click "My TGA": Term Grade Average

  1. TGA is calcolated across all courses taken in a term.

Click "My CGA": Cumolative Grade Average

  1. CGA is calcolated for all the courses earning grade points completed since first registration in a program up to the point of calcolation.

Click "My GGA": Graduation Cumolative Grade Average

  1. GGA is the student's CGA adjusted by excluding courses and adopting a weighting policy under established University's roles.
